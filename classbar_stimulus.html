◆ startPosition

Property baseStimulus::startPosition
inherited

For moving stimuli do we start "before" our initial position? THis allows you to center a stimulus at a screen location, but then drift it across that location, so if xyPosition is 0,0 and startPosition is -2 then the stimulus will start at -2 drifing towards 0.
